# Colorado

Status: current-law

## Enacted Law

- HB26-1144 became law as Chapter 94, Session Laws of Colorado 2026.
- The act prohibits knowingly manufacturing or producing a potentially functioning firearm, unfinished frame or receiver, large-capacity magazine, or rapid-fire device by three-dimensional printing.
- The act treats three-dimensional printing as additive and subtractive manufacturing.
- Exceptions include federally licensed firearm manufacturers, accredited gunsmithing programs, and instructors or students in those programs.
