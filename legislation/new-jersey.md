# New Jersey

Status: current-law

## Enacted Law

- N.J.S. 2C:39-9(l) makes it a crime for an unlicensed person to use a 3D printer or similar device to manufacture or produce a firearm, firearm receiver, magazine, or firearm component.
- The same subsection prohibits distributing digital instructions to an unlicensed person in New Jersey when those instructions may be used to program a 3D printer to produce covered firearms or components.
- A4975, passed January 12, 2026, added a crime for possessing digital instructions to illegally manufacture firearms and firearm components.
