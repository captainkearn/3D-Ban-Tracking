# Massachusetts

Status: current-law

## Enacted Law

- Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 140, Section 121D regulates 3D printers and CNC milling machines used to manufacture or assemble firearms.
- Section 121D prohibits use of a 3D printer or CNC milling machine to manufacture or assemble a firearm in Massachusetts without a valid license to carry firearms.
- Related provisions also restrict sale or transfer of machines that have a primary or intended function of manufacturing or assembling firearms.
