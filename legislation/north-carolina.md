# North Carolina

Status: active-proposal

Last checked: 2026-07-16.

## Summary

- HB 545 / S 409 would ban ghost guns and undetectable firearms.
- HB 545 was filed March 26, 2025, passed first reading, and was referred to the House Rules, Calendar, and Operations Committee on March 31, 2025.
- The bill summary says it would make it a Class I felony to manufacture, sell, give away, transfer, use, or possess a ghost gun or undetectable firearm, with exceptions for licensed firearm manufacturers.
- The North Carolina Legislative Reporting Service summary states the bill defines an undetectable firearm to include one manufactured through a 3D printing process.

## Notes

This is not a printer-sale blocking mandate. It is an active ghost-gun / undetectable-firearm proposal that expressly reaches firearms made through a 3D printing process.
