# Minnesota

Status: active-proposal

## Enacted Law

- No current state 3D-printing firearm ban was found in this sweep.

## Active Proposal

- SF 3661 / HF 3407 would prohibit the sale and possession of ghost guns, limit 3D printing of firearms to federally licensed firearms manufacturers, and prohibit distribution of 3D-printer firearm design files.
- As of the July 16, 2026 sweep, no new official movement was found beyond the March 2026 House/Senate activity and SF 3661 1st engrossment.
- The Senate file reached second reading after a committee report to pass as amended on March 18, 2026.
- The House file was introduced and referred to Public Safety Finance and Policy; third-party tracking showed the House version died in committee.
