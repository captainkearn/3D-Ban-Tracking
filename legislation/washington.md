# Washington

Status: passed-active

## Enacted Law

- HB 2320 became Chapter 203, Laws of 2026, and was signed by the governor on March 24, 2026.
- The bill regulates firearm manufacturing, including 3D-printed firearms and digital gun blueprints, and strengthens Washington's ghost-gun statute.
- The bill took effect March 24, 2026.

## Active Proposal

- HB 2321 would require 3D printers to be equipped with certain blocking technologies.
- As of the July 8, 2026 bill page snapshot, HB 2321 was in House Civil Rights & Judiciary after first reading and referral.
