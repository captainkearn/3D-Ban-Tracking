# Maine

Status: current-law

Last checked: 2026-07-16.

## Summary

- LD 1126 / Public Law 537 was enacted without the governor's signature on January 11, 2026.
- The law requires serial numbers on covered firearms, frames, receivers, and unfinished frames/receivers, and prohibits undetectable firearms.
- The chaptered law expressly defines "three-dimensional printer" and defines "unfinished frame or receiver" to include printing, extrusion, machined bodies, and similar articles that may readily become frames or receivers.
- Maine's violations provisions begin January 1, 2027.

## Notes

This is not a printer-sale blocking mandate. It is a ghost-gun / serialization / undetectable-firearm law that expressly references three-dimensional printers and printed unfinished frames or receivers.
