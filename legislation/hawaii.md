# Hawaii

Status: current-law

## Enacted Law

- Hawaii Revised Statutes Section 134-10.2 bars a person who is not licensed to manufacture firearms, or who is not a federally licensed dealer, from producing with a 3D printer certain unserialized firearm receivers or parts for the purpose of assembling a firearm.
- A violation is a class C felony.
