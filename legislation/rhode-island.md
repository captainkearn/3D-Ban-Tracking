# Rhode Island

Status: current-law

## Enacted Law

- R.I. Gen. Laws Section 11-47-8(e) prohibits manufacturing, selling, offering to sell, transferring, purchasing, possessing, or having under one's control a ghost gun, an undetectable firearm, or any firearm produced by a 3D printing process.
- The prohibition does not apply to federally licensed manufacturers with ATF Type 07 licenses.
- R.I. Gen. Laws Section 11-47-2 defines "3D printing process" and includes 3D-printed firearms in the undetectable-firearm definition.
